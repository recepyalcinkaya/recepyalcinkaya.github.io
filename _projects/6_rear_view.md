An essential spatial awareness system developed to assist the driver during parking and reverse maneuvers.

The system processes live rear camera feeds and distance metrics from rear-mounted sensors. It is seamlessly integrated into the central Linux (Ubuntu) operating environment of the vehicle, projecting dynamic guidelines and distance warnings directly onto the cabin display.
